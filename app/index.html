Color Factory is an interactive exhibit that celebrates the discovery, serendipity and generosity of color.

Color Factory opens in New York City, NYC, August 2018, bringing a whole new palette to a 20,000 square foot space in SoHo, featuring a roster of talented artists, designers and makers who will tell their unique color stories and engage the senses in unexpected ways. Inspired by the colors of the city, this temporary exhibit invites visitors to experience 16 installations designed to awaken audiences to the brilliant, everyday presence of color.
